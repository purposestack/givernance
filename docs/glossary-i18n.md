# Givernance — Domain Glossary (FR/EN)

> Translation reference for the Translation Specialist agent and human translators.
> **Source language**: French (primary market). **Target**: English.
> Maintained alongside `messages/fr.json` and `messages/en.json`.

## Core Domain Terms

| French (source) | English | Context / Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Constituant | Constituent | Any person or org in the CRM (donor, volunteer, beneficiary, partner). NOT "contact" or "member". |
| Don | Donation | A financial contribution. NOT "gift" (too informal for NPO context). |
| Donateur / Donatrice | Donor | Person who makes a donation. |
| Campagne | Campaign | Fundraising campaign (`campagne de collecte`), NOT marketing campaign. |
| Subvention | Grant | Funding from institutional source (foundation, government). |
| Bénévole | Volunteer | Person giving unpaid time. |
| Bénéficiaire | Beneficiary | Person or community served by the NPO's programs. |
| Bailleur | Funder | Organization providing grants (foundation, government agency). |
| Programme | Program | A structured set of activities the NPO runs. |
| Impact | Impact | Measurable social outcome. Keep untranslated in both languages. |

| French | English | Context |
|---|---|---|

| Déduction d'impôt | Tax deduction | Tax benefit for donors. |
| Don récurrent | Recurring donation | Scheduled repeated donation (monthly, yearly). |
| Prélèvement automatique | Direct debit | SEPA DD payment method. |
| Virement | Bank transfer | Payment via wire transfer. |
| Rapprochement | Reconciliation | Matching payments to expected amounts. |
| Exercice fiscal | Fiscal year | Accounting year period. |
| Montant | Amount | Financial value. |

## Auth Terms

| French | English | Context |
|---|---|---|
| Se connecter | Sign in | NOT "Log in" (too technical for NPO staff). |
| Se déconnecter | Sign out | NOT "Log out". |

## Formatting Conventions

| Concern | French | English |
|---|---|---|
| Currency | `12 450 €` (space thousands, € after) | `€12,450` (comma thousands, € before) |
| Date | `17 avril 2026` or `17/04/2026` | `April 17, 2026` or `04/17/2026` |
| Percentage | `18 %` (space before %) | `18%` (no space) |
| Thousands separator | space (`1 247`) | comma (`1,247`) |
| Decimal separator | comma (`3,50 €`) | period (`€3.50`) |

## Tone & Register

- **French**: Formal-but-warm (`vous` form, never `tu`). Professional yet approachable. Use active voice. Avoid jargon that small NPO staff wouldn't know.
- **English**: Clear-and-direct. Professional but not corporate. UK English spelling (`organisation`, `programme`) for EU market alignment.
